About

Hi and thanks for visiting Blitz Analog! I'm Trenton Blitz Bricken and love film photography.

Every image you see here has been taken with either the Leica M3, Rolleiflex 2.8f, or Fuji GSW690III. All photos are minimally edited because I care a lot about maximizing authenticity and letting my choice of film doing all the editing I need. However, I do sometimes need to remove dust while scanning and adjust for exposure and contrast. Most of these images are also developed and scanned by me in my personal darkroom.

I am based in Boston and but also frequent London and travel quite a bit in general (as you can probably infer). Please contact me via email here or reach out through Instagram to order prints, arrange for photoshoots, or just to say hello :).
